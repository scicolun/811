About the Department


The Department of Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) follows the National Education Policy (NEP 2020) curriculum of Hemchandracharya North Gujarat University (HNGU). The department is committed to providing quality education with modern laboratories, experienced faculty, and practical-based learning to prepare students for higher studies, research, and professional careers.

Why Choose B.Sc.?


  • NEP 2020 curriculum implemented under HNGU.
  • Major, Minor and Multidisciplinary course options.
  • Modern laboratories with practical-based education.
  • Experienced teaching faculty.
  • Library and digital learning resources.
  • Seminars, workshops and science exhibitions.
  • Preparation for higher education and competitive examinations.
  • Focus on research, innovation and scientific thinking.

Eligibility


Candidates who have passed the Higher Secondary Examination (10+2) in the Science stream from a recognized board are eligible for admission to the B.Sc. programme, subject to the admission rules of Hemchandracharya North Gujarat University (HNGU) and the college.
