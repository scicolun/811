About the Department


The Department of Commerce offers the Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com.) Programme under the National Education Policy (NEP 2020) of Hemchandracharya North Gujarat University (HNGU). The department aims to provide students with a strong foundation in commerce, accounting, business management, finance and entrepreneurship while promoting ethical values, professional competence and lifelong learning.

Vision & Mission


Vision


To provide quality commerce education that develops professional competence, ethical values, leadership skills and prepares students for higher education, entrepreneurship and successful careers.


Mission


  • Provide quality commerce education under NEP 2020.
  • Develop accounting and financial management skills.
  • Promote entrepreneurship and innovation.
  • Encourage ethical business practices.
  • Prepare students for employment and higher studies.
  • Develop leadership and communication skills.
