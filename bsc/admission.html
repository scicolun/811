Admission Overview


Admissions to the Bachelor of Science (B,Sc/BA/BCom.) Programme are conducted through the Government of Gujarat Common Admission Services (GCAS) portal. Students seeking admission must complete the online registration process, upload the required documents, select the preferred college and programme, and participate in the centralized admission process as per Government of Gujarat and Hemchandracharya North Gujarat University (HNGU) guidelines.

Important: Admission to the B,Sc/BA/BCom. Programme is processed only through the official GCAS Portal. Students are advised to regularly check the GCAS portal and the college website for updates regarding registration dates, merit lists, counselling, fee payment, and reporting schedule.

Eligibility Criteria


  • Passed Higher Secondary Examination (10+2) from a recognized Board.
  • Science Stream with subjects as prescribed by HNGU.
  • Admission strictly according to Government of Gujarat & HNGU rules.
  • Students must complete registration through the GCAS Portal.
  • Original documents must be produced during verification.

GCAS Admission Process


Step 1 - Visit GCAS Portal

Open the Government of Gujarat Common Admission Services (GCAS) portal.

Step 2 - Student Registration

Create your account using your mobile number, email address and Aadhaar details.

Step 3 - Verify Mobile Number

Verify your mobile number using the OTP received from GCAS.

Step 4 - Fill Personal Details

Enter your personal, academic and communication details carefully.

Step 5 - Upload Documents

Upload all required documents in the prescribed format.

Step 6 - Select University

Select Hemchandracharya North Gujarat University (HNGU).

Step 7 - Select College

Select Shri Un Vibhag Kelvani Mandal Sanchalit Science College, Un.

Step 8 - Choose Programme

Select Bachelor of Science (B,Sc/BA/BCom.) and your preferred subject combination.

Step 9 - Lock Choices

Verify all information and lock your choices before the deadline.

Step 10 - Merit List

Wait for the official merit list published through GCAS.

Step 11 - Fee Payment

Pay the admission fee within the prescribed time after seat allotment.

Step 12 - Report to College

Visit the college with all original documents for final verification and confirmation of admission.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)


1. Is admission completely online?

Yes. Students must register through the Government of Gujarat Common Admission Services (GCAS) Portal. The final document verification and fee confirmation are completed at the college.


2. Can I apply directly to the college?

No. Admission is carried out through the GCAS centralized admission process.


3. Which documents should I bring during verification?

Bring all original academic certificates, Aadhaar Card, School Leaving Certificate, passport photographs, caste certificate (if applicable), income certificate (if applicable), and any other documents required by GCAS or HNGU.


4. How will I know if I am selected?

Students should regularly check the GCAS Portal and the College Website for merit lists and admission notifications.


5. When should I pay the admission fees?

After seat allotment through GCAS, fees must be paid within the prescribed time limit.
