About the Department


The Department of Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) follows the National Education Policy (NEP 2020) curriculum of Hemchandracharya North Gujarat University (HNGU). The department is committed to developing critical thinking, communication skills, ethical values, creativity and social awareness through quality education in humanities and social sciences.

Vision & Mission


Vision


To provide quality education in Arts and Humanities that develops knowledgeable, responsible and socially committed citizens capable of contributing positively to society.


Mission


  • Provide quality education under NEP 2020.
  • Develop communication and analytical skills.
  • Promote ethical values and social responsibility.
  • Encourage research, creativity and innovation.
  • Prepare students for higher education and competitive examinations.
  • Support overall personality development.
